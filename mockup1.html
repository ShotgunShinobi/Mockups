Questions your visitors will ask first.

Who is this site structure best for?

Certification coaches, boutique consultancies, training institutes, and subject-matter-led education businesses.

Should I keep it one page or multi-page?

Start with one strong homepage, then add dedicated pages for programs, about, and contact once the messaging is validated.

What should the main CTA be?

Use one high-intent action such as register interest, book a free consultation, or request a corporate proposal.
