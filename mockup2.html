Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the PMP® Certification package?

Our comprehensive package includes 35 hours of live instructor-led training, a dedicated student portal with recorded sessions, 5 full-length mock simulators, application review assistance, and a personalized study plan leading up to your exam day.

Do you offer customized corporate training formats?

Yes. We tailor our curriculum to align with your organization's specific delivery frameworks (Agile, Waterfall, or Hybrid). Workshops can be delivered on-site, virtually, or in a hybrid format over multiple weeks.

What happens if I fail the exam on the first try?

We offer a success guarantee. If you complete our entire program, score above 80% on our final mock exams, and do not pass the actual exam, we provide continuous 1-on-1 coaching and unlimited access to future cohorts until you succeed.
